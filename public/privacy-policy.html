Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Unmarkly. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our free Sora AI watermark remover service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, operating system, and device information for security and service optimization.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our watermark removal service

4. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is stored securely in encrypted databases
  • We do not store the video files you process
  • Video processing happens in real-time, and no content is permanently saved
  • Rate limit and behavioral data is automatically purged after 24 hours

5. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use traditional cookies. However, we may use local storage and similar technologies to maintain service functionality, including device fingerprinting for security purposes.

6. Third-Party Services

These services have their own privacy policies and may collect data independently.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Security logs: 30 days

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of our service at any time

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy
