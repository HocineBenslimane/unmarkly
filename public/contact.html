Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use Unmarkly?

Simply paste the Sora AI video URL from sora.chatgpt.com into the input field on our homepage and click "Remove Watermark". Your video will be processed and ready for download in seconds.

Why am I seeing a rate limit error?

To prevent abuse, we limit each user to 3 downloads per 24-hour period. If you've reached this limit, please wait for the timer to reset before trying again.

Is the service really free?

Yes! Unmarkly is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or payment requirements.

What video quality will I get?

We provide the original HD quality of the Sora AI video without any compression or quality loss during the watermark removal process.

Do you store my videos?

No. All video processing happens in real-time, and we do not store any video content on our servers. Your privacy is important to us.

Can I use downloaded videos commercially?

You must respect the original copyright and terms of service from OpenAI Sora. Unmarkly only provides a download tool - you are responsible for ensuring you have the right to use the content.
